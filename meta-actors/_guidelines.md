# Characters guideline

Strength:
0 - dead
5 - normal person
10 - somewhat strong or dexterous
50 - limits of human strength

Magic ability:
0 - normal person
5 - can only use the simplest spells
10 - from this point people are considered "mages"
...
50 - rare, allows to use all "normal" spells if alignment allows
100 - allows to perform all spells of your alignment

Speed:
100 - normal

Mastery:
0 - no experience, basice damage output
1 - expert, allows to deal double damage with certain effects
2 - master, triples damage output

Stat gains (STR, MAG, SPD)
1, 1, 1 - normal person
0, 2, 1 - magic inclined person
2, 0, 1 - person without interest in magic
0, X>2, 0 - magic obsessed person or a person with particular disposition to to it

Energy:
0 energy - can't perform anything particularly interesting
5 energy - allows to perform normal spells and all attack-related active skills
10 energy - allows to perform all normal spells
20 energy - can perform everything

HP:
100 hp - base amount, normal person
modified by strength and alignment
